Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon until crispy.

2. Butter one side of each bread slice.

3. Place a slice of bread, butter side down, in a skillet over medium heat.

4. Add cheese, bacon, tomato slices, and another slice of bread on top, butter side up.

5. Cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted.

6. Serve hot and enjoy!
