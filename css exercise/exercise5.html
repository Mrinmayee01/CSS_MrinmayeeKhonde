Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 12 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 8 slices bread
  • 4 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towels.

2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of each bread slice.

4. Place a slice of tomato on top of the cheese on half of the bread slices.

5. Place 2 slices of bacon on top of the tomato slices.

6. Top with the remaining bread slices, buttered side up.

7. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place sandwiches on the skillet or griddle and cook until golden brown on each side and cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

8. Serve hot and enjoy!
